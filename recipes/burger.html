Burger

Originating in Germany, this dish has proven best accompanied by a cold lager and the company of your best friends. Enjoy the rich aromas of the fresh hops and indulge in the smokiness of the patty while washing it all down with a pint of your favorite beer.

Ingredients

  • Angus patty
  • Brioche buns
  • Secret Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Grill patty
  2. Prepare the secret sauce
  3. Toast the buns
  4. Slice the cheese
