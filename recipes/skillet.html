Skewer

Originating in Africa, this dish has proven best accompanied by a fresh barley beer and the company of a friend. Enjoy the fresh aromas of the beer and indulge in the smoky smell os the meat while accompanying it with a cup of an aromatic barley beer.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Stick
  • Veggies
  • Beer

Steps

  1. Grill meat
  2. Grill Veggies
  3. Brew beer
  4. Skewer meats and veggies
