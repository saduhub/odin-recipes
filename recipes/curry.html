Curry

Originating in India, this dish has proven best accompanied by Jasmine tea and the company of a friend. Enjoy the spicy aromas of the curry and indulge in the complex array of spices while accompanying it with a cup of an aromatic Jasmine tea.

Ingredients

  • Curry
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Tea

Steps

  1. Prepare curry
  2. Prepare rice
  3. Brew tea
  4. Add curry to rice and chicken
