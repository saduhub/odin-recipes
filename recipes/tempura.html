Tempura

Originating in Japan, this dish has proven best accompanied by mild sake and the company of colleagues. Enjoy the strong taste of the sake and indulge in the crispy texture of tempura shrimp, vegetables, and calamari.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Breading
  • Base
  • Sake

Steps

  1. Make batter
  2. Cover base in batter
  3. Chill sake
  4. Deep-fry
